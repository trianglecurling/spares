# Registration Launch Readiness Checklist

This checklist is the Phase 10 sign-off artifact for registration launch readiness.
It does not replace automated tests. It records the manual Stripe, data, staff,
and production-readiness checks that must be completed before opening registration.

## Status Legend

- `[ ]` Not started
- `[~]` In progress or partially verified
- `[x]` Verified
- `[!]` Failed or launch blocker
- `[A]` Accepted launch exception

## 2. Stripe Test-Mode Verification

Run these in Stripe test mode before production launch.

- [ ] Immediate payment succeeds and confirms registration.
- [ ] Immediate payment fails and leaves registration unpaid/unconfirmed.
- [ ] Checkout is abandoned or cancelled and leaves registration unpaid/unconfirmed.
- [ ] Duplicate success webhook does not duplicate confirmation, payment, membership, or email effects.
- [ ] Deferred payment link is generated by staff with the correct amount.
- [ ] Deferred payment succeeds and updates payment/registration status.
- [ ] Deferred payment fails and remains visible to staff.
- [ ] Resending a deferred payment link does not corrupt invoice or registration state.
- [ ] Cancelled registration cannot be confirmed by an old payment link.
- [ ] Stripe amount matches the server-calculated invoice total.

## 4. Production Data Validation

- [ ] Fiscal year is configured for July 1 through June 30.
- [ ] Active season and sessions are configured.
- [ ] Registration state schedule is configured and initially closed.
- [ ] Regular, social, spare-only, sabbatical, Junior Recreational, and league fees are configured.
- [ ] Student, reciprocal, and winter-only discounts are configured.
- [ ] League capacities, first day of play, and last day of play are configured.
- [ ] Age and experience requirements are valid.
- [ ] BYOT leagues use team capacity and do not allow waitlists or sabbaticals.
- [ ] Standard leagues that should use waitlists have waitlists enabled.
- [ ] League predecessor/successor links are configured and non-circular.
- [ ] Prior league participation, sabbaticals, and existing waitlists are imported or verified.
- [ ] User profile data quality issues are identified, especially missing email, date of birth, emergency contact, and guardian fields.
- [ ] Test registrations are absent from production.

## 6. Launch Steps

Before opening registration:

- [ ] Confirm registration is closed.
- [ ] Confirm production configuration, pricing, discounts, and league settings.
- [ ] Confirm Stripe live mode and webhook endpoint.
- [ ] Confirm email sending is configured.
- [ ] Confirm staff roles are assigned.
- [ ] Confirm backup/export of relevant data exists.
- [ ] Confirm staff rehearsal is complete.
- [ ] Confirm known issues are classified.

Go-live:

1. Announce expected registration opening.
2. Enable monitoring/log review.
3. Open registration or allow scheduled transition.
4. Submit one controlled real registration if appropriate.
5. Verify payment, email, registration status, and staff visibility.
6. Monitor errors and support requests.
7. Keep staff and technical support available during the first registration window.

## 7. Pause and Contingency Plan

Pause registration immediately for serious issues such as incorrect pricing,
incorrect eligibility decisions, payment failures affecting multiple users,
unauthorized data access, corrupted waitlists, or webhook failures affecting
confirmation.

When paused:

- Existing submitted registrations remain intact.
- New starts/submissions are blocked by the configured registration state.
- Members see a clear temporary-unavailable message.
- Staff continues reviewing existing registrations only if safe.

Classifications:
